    KAI VENICE

    Kai Venice is an Italian young singer. His inspirations come from more artists in the industry of music. His name contains the city Venice, which he really loves and goes often due to family, and the name Kai was taken by himself because he liked the name personally. Today Kai works with Bandlab to produce vocals into instrumentals and created his own community to collaborate and feature with more producers.

    HOW DID IT START?

    Kai always had a passion for music, even when he was a kid. He liked Pop and Alternative songs, but didn't really know it. At the age of 10, he started to follow a really great artist in the industry of music. That artist opened a world for him, and he started to follow more artists and listen to more genres. At the age of 13, he made a decision; he wanted to start making his own music, where he only covered songs from different artists. Unfortunately, all the copies are unreleased and went missing, except one: Cut my Hair (Cover).

    PORTFOLIO

    This site is a Gallery made by Kai. He wanted to add a Portfolio and he made some Photoshoots to put them into the Website.

    KAI'S WORDS

    "I always liked Photoshoots so I thought it would be cool to try it one time and I think it was worth it. I really liked the vibe and I am very happy to share the photos with you all now!"
